About this site

Welcome to Zim Left Radio, a podcast dedicated to left-wing analysis of Zimbabwe’s economy and society

Day in day and day out we are told Zimbabwe’s sociol-economic problems are a result of economic mismanagement and corruption.

Or, if you are aligned to the ruling party, Zimbabwe’s problems are a result of sanctions.

Whilst both arguments are probably partially correct, I argue that they do not give a complete picture.

In fact there remains many open questions, for example, if sanctions are that bad why did Rhodesia and more recently Russia do well under economic sanctions?

If mismanagement is indeed the problem, with Mthuli Ncube, a Cambridge graduate and Oxford professor as finance minister why is Zimbabwe’s economy not improving? Surely there can be no better managers than an Oxford professor or are there any?

In this podcast, I will explore many topics using a Marxist-Leninist perspective to give you the listener an alternative view!
